About Me

I'm a web developer in training, currently attending the UTD Fullstack Academy Web Developer Boot Camp. I have a background in customer service and delivery, and I'm passionate about learning new technologies and building responsive, accessible websites.

I enjoy working on projects that help people, and I bring a unique perspective thanks to my life experiences, including time spent mentoring others in recovery programs. I'm especially interested in front-end development, user experience design, and making clean, easy-to-navigate interfaces.
